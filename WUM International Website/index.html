About

WUM International is an Export and Import facilitator and a trading body for all kinds of meat and frozen food products to and from India. Supported by leading professionals of the Meat Industry in India and guided by highly experienced corporates of India; WUM International aims for the highest customer satisfaction by providing the best quality frozen and processed food products of all kinds; to, and from, India.
We do, arrange and manage procurement, production, logistics and sales with marketing strategies for clients. Our biggest strength is the blend of the energy of youth and the gift of experience.

Import

We are into imports of frozen foods and cold cuts from around the world as per our clients’ requirements. We provide the highest quality assurance and trustworthy products.

Export

We export Best Quality Rice and Frozen and Chilled Halal Indian Buffalo Meat to buyers from countries like China, Middle East, etc. We facilitate export of any other products that can be exported from India as well.

Retail

With our team’s expertise in supply chain, we supply frozen food items in organised and disorganised markets to distributors, dealers, large retail stores as well as small retailers.

What We Do

International Business And Trade

The world is becoming a smaller place with the ease of connectivity. With every country and individual working towards growth, we facilitate the food trade through our insights and efforts. We believe in quality, term goals and a result oriented approach to help you create a cost effective supply chain.
We trade in Halal Frozen Boneless Buffalo Meat, Frozen Meat Products and Chicken Leg cut pieces etc.. We are also into exports from and imports to India of a variety of Food Products, frozen and otherwise.

Sourcing and Sales

With the best possible connect to all types and processes of the food industry, our team is best equipped to deliver quality service to help our clients capitalize on their strengths; assisting them in various stages of their exports or imports; from start to finish; or; in providing the best possible goods at the best rates. With our contacts and experience in the Industry; we have an able understanding of the Indian market. We believe in quality, term goals and a result oriented approach to help you create a cost effective supply chain.
